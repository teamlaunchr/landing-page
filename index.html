launchR

the student-run startup accelerator @ rutgers university

Who we are

launchR is the Rutgers student-driven clean energy startup accelerator funded by the United States Department of Energy.

What we do

Our program is cultivating the next generation of green energy and clean technology entrepreneurs by connecting young, budding innovators with access to strategic networks, capital, and essential resources.
